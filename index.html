OHIO Late Nights

The Department of Housing and Residence Life presents OHIO Late Nights, an alcohol alternative program. Residents living on Ohio University's campus attend for a fun filled evening. The Late Nights event occurs every Saturday satarting September 5th. Late Nights happens rain or shine from 9 pm - 1 am at our new facility, the Living Learning Center, on South Green.

Activities include:

  • Games
  • Karoke
  • Music
  • Movies
  • Snacks
  • and More
