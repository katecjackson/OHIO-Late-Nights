About

The Department of Housing and Residence Life presents OHIO Late Nights, an alcohol alternative program. Residents living on Ohio University's campus can attend this event for a fun filled evening. Late Nights occurs every Saturday satarting September 5th, 2015. The event happens rain or shine from 9 pm - 1 am at our new facility, the Living Learning Center, on South Green.

Activities include:

Games

Karoke

Music

Movies

Snacks

and more!
